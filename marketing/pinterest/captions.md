```
A RED oil pressure warning is one of the most urgent dashboard lights.

What to do:
1. Pull over and turn off the engine
2. Check oil level when safe (dipstick)
3. Do NOT keep driving if the light stays on

Low oil pressure can damage the engine quickly — towing beats limping to a shop.

## Pin 3 — TPMS / Tire Pressure

**File:** `pinterest-tpms-tire-pressure.png`  
**Destination link:** https://warninglightfinder.com/symbols/tire-pressure-warning-light/

**Title:**
```
TPMS Light — Horseshoe Exclamation Mark on Dashboard
```

**Description:**
```
That horseshoe / U-shape with “!” is usually your tire pressure monitoring system (TPMS).

Quick steps:
• Check all four tire pressures (door jamb placard — not the max on the tire sidewall)
• Cold weather can drop PSI — inflate when tires are cold
• Stop if a tire looks flat or the car pulls strongly

Full TPMS guide:
